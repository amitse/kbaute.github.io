Hello!
I'm going to be sealing myself in a homemade air-tight bio-dome for 3 days some time between October 22nd-28th, 2018. It'll be a 10ft x 10ft x 10ft clear-plastic cube filled with plants, a bunch of air sensors, some cameras, a hammock, a bucket (...), and me. If all goes well with this experiment the plants will make the air breathable for me and in exchange I'll turn food into CO2 for them.

I will be making three videos in there about the environmental processes that allow for us to breathe and survive, and also the ways we humans impact those processes. In addition those videos, I want this experiment to reach as many people as possible, and I'm also hoping to keep from going insane in there on my own, so... lets make some science!

I would love to collaborate on videos and podcasts about this - anything Health, Space, Environmental Science, Psychology, or Biology goes!

Contact me at kurtis.baute@gmail.com with an idea or a topic of interest as well as a link to your channel or podcast. I make no garuantee's I'll be able to take on everyone's projects, but I will try my best to respond to everyone!
